Welcome to Ulster Sports Club

Since opening our doors in 2001, Ulster Club has been a hub for aspiring athletes and sports lovers alike. What started as a small soccer group has blossomed into a vibrant, multi-sport community center that supports thousands of athletes every year.

Our goal? To help every athlete—no matter their age or skill level—reach their potential through expert coaching, top-tier facilities, and a strong, supportive community that champions teamwork, dedication, and personal growth.

What You Can Play Here

Soccer

Join our year-round leagues—whether you're looking to compete or just play for fun. Youth and adult teams welcome!

Basketball

From skill-building programs for kids to high-energy adult leagues—we have got hoops action for everyone.

Baseball/Softball

Take the field in spring or summer leagues, or train with us during winter to sharpen your game.

Volleyball

Serve, spike, and block year-round in our indoor or beach volleyball programs for all ages.
